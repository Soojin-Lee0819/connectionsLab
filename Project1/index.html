What's Your Name?

There's a lot to a name. Where is Soojin from? How old is Fischer? Hover to find out what data says about these names and click PLAY to start the game.

Collect Names Around the World

Type in a name and hit submit! The predicted age and country of each name will be displayed. The predicted age will be added to your total ‘Age Score’. When the collective Age Score is above 500, you win! But there’s a catch: if you enter two names from the same country, you lose!
